Serial killers don't just hatch overnight, they are the product of years of circumstances and thought processes which makes killing the one thing that drives them forward. This page will provide information about the many mental illnesses and personality disorders common among these offenders, as well as provide help for victims and families to help keep them safe from kidnappers, child molesters and serial killers. We as a community need to know our enemy in order to educate our children on how to stay safe. This subject may not be the easiest to digest but it has been one of much interest, so if you want to help solve real life murder cases, then take the time to learn more about the types of disorders that often motivates these individuals to keep killing.
